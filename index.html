About me

David Doo is a software engineer who works with development teams (corporate and startups) to discover new web solutions and deliver beautiful applications to customers. David knows that computers and applications can be daunting, and presents such abstract concepts in a familiar way. He helps clients fulfill the product of their dreams and sees through the building process from conception to production.

David acquired a Software Development internship at Amazon.com for Summer 2019, where he improved how customers interact with the Amazonian social media experience. He also maintains a graveyard of a multitude of GitHub side projects, which he swears that they're all 'the next big thing'.

He is also currently pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science at SUNY Stony Brook University. In his free time, he immerses himself in music by going to shows, concerts, and dance workshops.
